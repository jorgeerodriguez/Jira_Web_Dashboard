Intake definition. project = DEVOPS, status in Triage or Reviewing — work that has not yet been accepted by an engineer. Ordered by created ascending. Data as of July 1, 2026.
Aging. Days since creation. A ticket is flagged aging at ≥5 days and stale at ≥7 — the signal to vet or reject before it rots.
Suggested owner (unassigned rows). SME = deepest domain history — top closer in the ticket’s primary domain, from a keyword tagger over 656 ticket titles — 612 completed + 44 active, completed weighted higher and To-Do excluded (63% matched; titles only; niche domains outrank broad ones like GitLab/Kubernetes). SMEs can be hand-set per domain (WorkSpaces = Adam) — a manual override that beats the tagger. Stretch = a domain runner-up with spare capacity (someone growing into it), else the most-free engineer overall. Both advisory; generic tickets fall back to capacity only.
Caveat. Capacity is a throughput proxy, not effort — it doesn't know a ticket's size or which engineer owns the relevant system. Treat the suggestion as a starting point, then apply judgment.

Next-30-day forecast

Central starts from a recency-weighted average of the last 3 complete months (Apr×1 + May×2 + Jun×3)/6, then blends toward the current month’s pace as the month progresses; range = ±1 s.d. of those months. Reflects recent pace and how this month is tracking. Shows for the members you've selected.

What's counted. project = DEVOPS, issue types Story / Task / Bug / Hotfix / Sub-task. "Completed" = the ticket entered the Done status (from the changelog; resolutiondate is unreliable here). "Will Not Do" excluded. Re-opens de-duplicated — each ticket counts once, in its first-completion month. Attributed by current assignee. Data as of July 1, 2026.
Forecast method. Central is a recency-weighted 3-month average (Apr–Jun, June heaviest) blended toward the current month’s run-rate — this month’s completions so far, extrapolated over the business days elapsed — weighted by how far into the month we are. Early in the month it tracks the recent-months baseline; it converges to the actual as the month fills in, so the estimate updates day to day. The band is ±1 standard deviation of the recent months, recentered on the estimate. Directional only.
Team. 14 members of Platform Engineering (confirmed roster). Excludes non-team assignees who occasionally get DEVOPS tickets. Over Jan–Jun the team completed 616 tickets (delivery types, deduped).
Caveat. Ticket count is throughput, not effort or complexity — a bucket-creation task and a multi-week platform build each count as 1. Read across the team accordingly.
